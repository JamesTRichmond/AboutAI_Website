📊 Finance Unchained: GPT-Powered Assistant for Scenario-Driven Forecasting

Overview

Engaged with a mid-sized finance team struggling with Excel-bound forecasting. Built an LLM-based tool that ingested structured and unstructured data, then generated dynamic variance narratives and “what-if” scenarios using GPT.

Challenge

Time-consuming manual analysis, poor communication between departments, and lack of clarity in forecasts.

Approach

Designed and implemented a GPT-powered FP&A chatbot, used LangChain + FAISS to allow natural-language queries over documentation, connected to live financial data pipelines.

Result

45% decrease in time-to-decision, leadership could explore forecasts without technical gatekeeping, boosted stakeholder trust in finance team’s insight generation.

What We Learned

Data doesn’t just need to be available—it needs to be interpretable at the speed of thought.
